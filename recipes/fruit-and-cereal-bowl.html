Fruit and Cereal Bowl

5 minutes 449 kcal • 60g protein • 3g fat • 45g carbs

Original yield: 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 250g Protein Yogurt (Strawberry
  • flavor by YoPro)
  • 45g Milo Protein Cereal (or any
  • protein cereal)
  • 1 Serving Protein Powder (Vanilla
  • creme by ''Rule1'')
  • 30g Blueberries
  • 50g Chopped Strawberries

Instructions

  1. Step 1: In a bowl add 250g of protein Yogurt and 1 serving of protein powder then mix together.

  2. Step 2: Add 30g of blueberries, 50g of chopped strawberries &amp; finish off by adding 45g of milo protein cereal.
