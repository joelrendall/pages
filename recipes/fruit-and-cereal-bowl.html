Fruit and Cereal Bowl

Ingredients

  • 250 g Protein Yogurt (Strawberry flavor by YoPro)
  • 45 g Milo Protein Cereal (or any protein cereal)
  • Protein Powder (Vanilla creme by ''Rule1'')
  • 30 g Blueberries
  • 50 g Chopped Strawberries

Instructions

  1. In a bowl add 250g of protein Yogurt and 1 serving of protein powder then mix together.
  2. Add 30g of blueberries, 50g of chopped strawberries & finish off by adding 45g of milo protein cereal.
