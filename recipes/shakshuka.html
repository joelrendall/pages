Shakshuka

30 minutes 493 kcal • 30g protein • 24g fat • 32g carbs

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Onion, sliced
  • 1 Capsicum deseeded and sliced
  • 1-2 Red Chillies, deseeded and
  • sliced
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 1 tsp Coriander
  • 1 can of Italian Peeled Tomatoes (Brand: Annalisa)
  • 1 tsp Caster Sugar
  • 4 Eggs
  • Small bunch Parsley, roughly
  • chopped
  • 25g Feta

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Stir fry onion, peppers and chillies in oiled pan until they begin to soften. Season with Paprika, Cumin and Coriander.

  2. Step 2: Add in can of tomatoes, break up tomatoes and add sugar, mix well until liquid has reduced and then season.

  3. Step 3: Create 4 wells and crack eggs into each, cover the pan and cook eggs on low heat until whites are set.

  4. Step 4: Top eggs with chopped parsley and Feta.
