Hoisin Beef Noodles 4 Servings

6 minutes 323 kcal • 32g protein • 10g fat • 17g carbs

Original yield: 4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 500g Lean Beef Mince
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Onion, sliced
  • 2 crushed Garlic Cloves
  • 2 Cups Shredded Wombok
  • 1 Cup Shredded Carrot
  • 250g Chinese Egg Noodles (Brand:
  • Wokka)
  • 2 tbsp Oyster Sauce
  • 2 tbsp Hoisin Sauce
  • 1 tsp Dark Soy Sauce
  • 2 tsp White Vinegar
  • 2 Spring Onions Chopped
  • Toasted Sesame Seeds

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Heat vegetable oil in wok or pan until piping hot.

  2. Step 2: Add beef mince into wok and spread it out, allowing it to sear for 5-6 minutes until beef starts to char.

  3. Step 3: Using a spatula, lift the charred beef mince from the pan and toss it around.

  4. Step 4: Add onion and garlic and stir fry for a minute. Then, add wombok and carrot and toss until carrot has just wilted. Now set aside.

  5. Step 5: Cook noodles in boiling water for a minute, or until al dente (slightly firm in the middle).

  6. Step 6: Drain and add to the pan with the beef and turn the heat back on high.

  7. Step 7: Add oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, soy sauce and vinegar and toss until everythings combined.

  8. Step 8: Garnish with spring onions and sesame seeds and serve!
