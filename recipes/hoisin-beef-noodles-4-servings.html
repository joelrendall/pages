Hoisin Beef Noodles 4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 500 g Lean Beef Mince
  • 30 ml Vegetable Oil
  • 1.0 onion , sliced
  • 2.0 crushed Garlic Cloves
  • 480 ml Shredded Wombok
  • 240 ml Shredded Carrot
  • 250 g Chinese Egg Noodles
  • 30 ml Oyster Sauce
  • 30 ml Hoisin Sauce
  • 5 ml Dark Soy Sauce
  • 10 ml White Vinegar
  • 2.0 spring Onions Chopped
  • Toasted Sesame Seeds

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in wok or pan until piping hot.
  2. Add beef mince into wok and spread it out, allowing it to sear for 5-6 minutes until beef starts to char.
  3. Using a spatula, lift the charred beef mince from the pan and toss it around.
  4. Add onion and garlic and stir fry for a minute. Then, add wombok and carrot and toss until carrot has just wilted. Now set aside.
  5. Cook noodles in boiling water for a minute, or until al dente (slightly firm in the middle).
  6. Drain and add to the pan with the beef and turn the heat back on high.
  7. Add oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, soy sauce and vinegar and toss until everythings combined.
  8. Garnish with spring onions and sesame seeds and serve!
