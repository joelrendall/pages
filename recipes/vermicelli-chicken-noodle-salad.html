Vermicelli Chicken Noodle Salad

30 minutes 477 kcal • 27g protein • 6g fat • 72g carbs

Ingredients

  • 100g Chicken Breast (Weighed
  • Raw)
  • 1 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 Tbsp Fish Sauce
  • 2 Cloves Garlic Crushed
  • 1 Tsp Brown Sugar
  • 80g Vermicelli Rice Noodle
  • 40g Cucumber
  • 40g Carrot
  • 40g Bean Sprouts
  • 10g Coriander
  • 50g Spring Onion
  • 50g Light Sweet Chilli Sauce (Ayam)

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Marinade chicken breast in soy sauce, fish sauce, minced garlic and brown sugar (leave for at least 30 minutes).

  2. Step 2: Turn pan or BBQ all the way up and grill chicken until cooked with nice char.

  3. Step 3: Set aside and leave chicken to rest while you prepare noodles.

  4. Step 4: Boil the kettle and in a bowl pour the hot water over the noodles, leave the noodles to soak for 10 min and then drain and cool the noodles in the fridge

  5. Step 5: Get all the other salad ingredients ready, Pick the herbs, cut the spring onion fine, cut the cucumber thin and grate the carrots

  6. Step 6: Slice chicken and add to bowl with noodles and salad. Dress with the sweet chilli and lemon juice
