Brekky Burger

4 minutes349 kcal • 40g protein • 5g fat • 32g carbs

Ingredients

  • 100 g Extra Lean Beef Mince
  • 25 g Low Fat Cheese (Bega 50% less fat grated cheese)
  • 20 g Sliced Honey Leg Ham
  • 1.0 egg
  • 1.0 high Fibre Low GI Soft Round Roll (Coles)
  • 15 ml Sriracha
  • 15 g Baby Spinach

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Weigh out 100g of extra lean beef mince & form into two 50g patties.

  2. Step 2: Season the patties with salt and pepper then place 25g of low fat cheese in the middle of 1 patty.

  3. Step 3: Place other patty on top & press down on the edges to seal the patties together.

  4. Step 4: Cook the newly formed large patty on a non stick pan on high heat for 3-4 minutes on each side. (or using a normal pan with olive oil)

  5. Step 5: Using the same pan cook 1 egg, 20g of sliced honey leg ham and toast the inside of the halved bun.

  6. Step 6: Once everything is cooked it's time to build the burger! Grab the bread roll & add 1 tbsp of sriracha, 15g of baby spinach & the rest of the ingredients that were cooked in the steps above.
