Chicken Pesto Pasta

35 minutes 480 kcal • 51g protein • 10g fat • 44g carbs

Ingredients

  • 80g San Remo Pasta Pro
  • 70g Raw Chicken Breast sliced
  • 90g Fresh Basil
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 8g Roasted Pine Nuts
  • 1/2 Tsp Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • Garlic Herb Seasoning (Brand:
  • Bronte Blends - optional)
  • 60g Low Fat Cottage Cheese (blended)

Steps

  1. Season raw chicken strips with salt, pepper and garlic seasoning. Cook in hot pan, then set aside.
  2. . Cook 80g of protein pasta (as per instructions on the packaging)
  3. In a blender cup add 90g of fresh basil, 2 garlic cloves, 8g of roasted pine nuts, 1/2 tsp of lemon juice, 1/2 tbsp of olive oil, 1/2 tbsp garlic herb seasoning (optional) & water (the amount of water you use is up to you & your liking, we use about 1/3 of a cup) and blend. This is your macro friendly pesto sauce.
  4. Once the pasta is cooked strain out the pasta water then add the pasta to a bowl with 1/2 of the pesto sauce, 60g of blended low fat cottage cheese & mix it all together.
  5. Pour the pasta onto a plate & add the cooked chicken breast on top & enjoy!
