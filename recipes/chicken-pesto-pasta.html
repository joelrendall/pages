Chicken Pesto Pasta

Ingredients

  • 80 g San Remo Pasta Pro
  • 70 g Raw Chicken Breast sliced
  • 90 g Fresh Basil
  • 2.0 garlic Cloves
  • 8 g Roasted Pine Nuts
  • 2.5 ml Lemon Juice
  • 7.5 ml Olive Oil
  • Garlic Herb Seasoning
  • 60 g Low Fat Cottage Cheese (blended)

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Season raw chicken strips with salt, pepper and garlic seasoning. Cook in hot pan, then set aside.

  2. Step 2: . Cook 80g of protein pasta (as per instructions on the packaging)

  3. Step 3: In a blender cup add 90g of fresh basil, 2 garlic cloves, 8g of roasted pine nuts, 1/2 tsp of lemon juice, 1/2 tbsp of olive oil, 1/2 tbsp garlic herb seasoning (optional) & water (the amount of water you use is up to you & your liking, we use about 1/3 of a cup) and blend. This is your macro friendly pesto sauce.

  4. Step 4: Once the pasta is cooked strain out the pasta water then add the pasta to a bowl with 1/2 of the pesto sauce, 60g of blended low fat cottage cheese & mix it all together.

  5. Step 5: Pour the pasta onto a plate & add the cooked chicken breast on top & enjoy!
