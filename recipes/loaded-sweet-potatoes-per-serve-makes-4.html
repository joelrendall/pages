Loaded Sweet Potatoes (per serve, makes 4)

15 minutes 356 kcal • 33g protein • 13g fat • 26g carbs

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Small Onion, Chopped
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, Crushed
  • 500g Extra Lean Beef Mince
  • 1 Tsp Cumin
  • 1 Tbsp Paprika
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 Can Diced Tomatoes
  • 3-4 Sweet Potatoes (~750g)
  • 75g Mozzarella Cheese
  • Chopped spring onions
  • 4 Tbsp Greek Yoghurt

Steps

  1. 1 1. Heat oil on a medium size frying pan at medium- high heat. Add onion and garlic and sauté until soft. Add meat, use a wooden spoon to break it up and cook until it’s no longer pink. Add cumin, paprika, salt, pepper and tomato, mix well. Cook covered for 15 minutes.
  2. Peel and wash sweet potatoes and stab with fork, then microwave for 5 minutes or until cooked through.
  3. Cut sweet potatoes in half lengthwise, push in a little bit at the corners until they open up and fill with a little ground beef and top with cheese
  4. Grill in oven at 180 for 5-10 minutes or until cheese has melted.
  5. Top with chopped spring onions and a dollop of greek yoghurt. .
