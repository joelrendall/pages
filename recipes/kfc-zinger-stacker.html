KFC Zinger Stacker

18 minutes 582 kcal • 78g protein • 11g fat • 51g carbs

Ingredients

  • 2 x 100g fillets of chicken breast
  • 30g Corn Flakes
  • Franks Red Hot Sauce
  • Salt, Pepper, Paprika, Garlic
  • Powder, Onion Powder
  • 1 x Aldi Brioche Bun
  • 2 x Slices Swiss Cheese
  • 2 Tsp. Light Mayo
  • 2 Tsp. Sriracha
  • 1 Tsp. Light Sweet Chilli Sauce
  • Shredded Lettuce

Steps

  1. Season two 100g pieces of chicken breast with salt, pepper, paprika, garlic power and onion power
  2. 2. Prepare two dipping stations. One with just Franks Hot Sauce, and the other with crushed Corn Flakes and a dash of all the seasonings above.
  3. Dip each chicken fillet into the Franks first, and then coat with cornflakes in the other dipping station.
  4. Pop chicken in air fryer at 190 degrees Celsius for 16-18 minutes, or oven at 200 for same amount of time (keep an eye on them though, all air fryers and ovens are different) 5, Mix sriracha and light mayo for supercharged sauce
  5. Construction time. Atop toasted Aldi brioche bun with supercharge sauce, shredded lettuce, chicken #1, a slice of light swiss cheese, chicken #2, another slice of light Swiss cheese, and top off with a drizzle of light sweet chilli sauce. Enjoy!
