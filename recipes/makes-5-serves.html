Makes 5 serves

Ingredients

  • 1000 g Chicken Breast fillets
  • 30 g of Honey
  • 3.0 cloves of garlic
  • 70 g of White Miso Paste
  • 40 g Rice Wine Vinegar (mirin( 2 tsp Sesame Oil 200g Fresh Green Beans 1 Head of Broccoli 1/2 tbsp Chilli Flakes 3 Spring Onions Brown rice to serve 1 tbsp rice bran oil

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 180 degrees celsius (350F)

  2. Step 2: In a bowl, combine white miso paste, crushed garlic, honey, rice wine vinegar and sesame oil. Add chicken and coat thoroughly, and leave to marinate for at least 30 minutes.

  3. Step 3: Transfer chicken and marinade into baking dish and roast in oven for 15 minutes, stir the mixture then return to oven for another 15 minutes.

  4. Step 4: While chicken is cooking, steam the broccoli and beans.

  5. Step 5: Heat rice bran oil in wok or hot pan. Add extra crushed garlic and chilli flakes, then toss broccoli and beans in pan until glossy.

  6. Step 6: Cook 2 cups of brown rice while chicken is cooking

  7. Step 7: Serve chicken and vegetables over cooked rice, garish with sesame seeds and chopped spring onion,
