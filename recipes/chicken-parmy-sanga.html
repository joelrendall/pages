Chicken Parmy Sanga

20 minutes670 kcal • 67g protein • 16g fat • 55g carbs

Ingredients

  • 200 g Chicken Breast (Approx 1/2 Breast)
  • 10 ml Paprika, Onion Powder,
  • Garlic Powder, Salt, Pepper (to taste)
  • 1.0 panini Roll (Woolworths bakery
  • 95 g )
  • 25 g Mozzarella
  • 30 g Cornflakes
  • 1.0 egg
  • 100 ml Passata/Marinara Sauce Basil Leaves

Instructions

  1. Step 1: . Beat chicken breast until it’s flattened.

  2. Step 2: 2. Season with 2 tsp garlic salt, paprika, 1 tsp black pepper, onion powder

  3. Step 3: Crack egg into one bowl and fill another bowl with 30g crushed cornflakes and the same seasonings as above. Coat your chicken into the egg, then the cornflakes until it’s entirely coated

  4. Step 4: Spray with olive oil and air fry @ 200°C for 15-20 min

  5. Step 5: Add 2 tbsp of marinara and 25g of low-fat mozzarella to top of chicken and continue to air fry for 2-3 more minutes

  6. Step 6: Half panini & toast, add some marinara sauce, your chicken, some basil leaves
