The Buff Mac

15 minutes 435 kcal • 47g protein • 14g fat • 25g carbs

Ingredients

  • 120g 95/5 beef mince
  • Brioche buns with sesame seeds (Aldi)
  • 30g chopped lettuce
  • 20g chopped onion
  • 2 x light sliced cheese (Woolworths)
  • Sliced pickles
  • 2 tbsp Greek yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp White vinegar
  • 1 tsp Yellow mustard
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 20g Diced picked
  • 20g Diced Onion
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. To make your healthy and enhanced Big Mac sauce add the following ingredients to a bowl and mix: diced onion, diced pickle, 2 tbsp greek yoghurt (this seems weird but trust its good), 1 tbsp yellow mustard, 1 tbsp of white vinegar, 1 tsp paprika, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp salt and 1/2 tsp pepper
  2. Take 120g of 95 beef mince, using your hands roll into two equal balls
  3. Cook patties on screaming hot pan, seasoning with pinch of salt after you flip the patty. If you want you can smash the patties as shown in the video (this is for some extra crisp and colour, but still nice if you cook patties normally)
  4. Toast buns and half bun (for middle of Big Mac) in pan after you have taken your patties out.
  5. Time to assemble! Layer burger in this order: bottom bun, lettuce, onion, pickles, sauce, patty, middle bun, lettuce, onion, pickles, sauce, patty, top bun
