Miso Chicken Rice Bowl Makes 5 serves

30 minutes 570 kcal • 55g protein • 12g fat • 57g carbs

Original yield: Makes 5 serve

Ingredients

  • 1kg Chicken Breast fillets
  • 30g of Honey
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 70g of White Miso Paste
  • 40g Rice Wine Vinegar (mirin(
  • 2 tsp Sesame Oil
  • 200g Fresh Green Beans
  • 1 Head of Broccoli
  • 1/2 tbsp Chilli Flakes
  • 3 Spring Onions
  • Brown rice to serve
  • 1 tbsp rice bran oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees celsius (350F)
  2. In a bowl, combine white miso paste, crushed garlic, honey, rice wine vinegar and sesame oil. Add chicken and coat thoroughly, and leave to marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Transfer chicken and marinade into baking dish and roast in oven for 15 minutes, stir the mixture then return to oven for another 15 minutes.
  4. While chicken is cooking, steam the broccoli and beans.
  5. Heat rice bran oil in wok or hot pan. Add extra crushed garlic and chilli flakes, then toss broccoli and beans in pan until glossy.
  6. Cook 2 cups of brown rice while chicken is cooking
  7. Serve chicken and vegetables over cooked rice, garish with sesame seeds and chopped spring onion,
