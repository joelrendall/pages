Nutella Donuts 6 servings (per serve)

15 minutes 126 kcal • 4g protein • 6g fat • 13g carbs

Original yield: 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 40g Oat Flour (blended oats)
  • 1 Egg
  • 100g Vanilla Yoghurt (Brand: YoPro)
  • 1 Tbsp Stevia (or sweetener of
  • choice)
  • 1/2 Tsp Baking Soda
  • 90g Melted Nutella (15g on each
  • donut)
  • Monkfruit Sugar Free Icing Powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. Step 1: In a bowl add 40g of oat flour, 1 egg, 100g of vanilla yoghurt, 1 tbsp of stevia, 1/2 tsp of baking soda &amp; mix well to combine.

  2. Step 2: Pour the mixture into a non stick donut baking tray (or regular donut baking tray with non stick spray)

  3. Step 3: Bake in the oven at 180 degrees celsius for 15 minutes (let the donuts cool for 5 minutes before taking them out the donut tray)

  4. Step 4: Melt 90g of nutella in the microwave

  5. Step 5: Take the donuts out the baking tray &amp; lightly sprinkle sugar free icing powder across the donuts (optional)

  6. Step 6: Drizzle 15g of melted nutella across each donut (6 donuts in total)
