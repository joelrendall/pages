Cadbury Skillet Cookie

Ingredients

  • 25 g Light Butter
  • 15 ml of Monkfruit Sweetener
  • 1.0 egg
  • 30 g Plain Flour
  • 10 g Choc Chips
  • 2.5 ml Vanilla Extract
  • 2 g Baking Powder
  • 2.5 ml Bicarbonate Soda

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180
  2. 2. In a skillet, melt your butter over medium-high heat until it's a rich brown colour and smells nutty
  3. Pour butter into a separate bowl and add 1 tbsp of Monkfruit sweetener and mix through. Crack your egg into mixture and stir.
  4. Add the flour, baking powder and bicarbonate soda and give it a mix. Finally, add your vanilla extract and chocolate chips.
  5. Add cookie batter back into skillet and bake at 180 for 10-14 minutes depending on how gooey you like your cookies.
