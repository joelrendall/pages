Cadbury Skillet Cookie

14 minutes 260 kcal • 6g protein • 13g fat • 32g carbs

Ingredients

  • 25g Light Butter
  • 1 Tbsp of Monkfruit Sweetener
  • 1 Egg
  • 30g Plain Flour
  • 10g Choc Chips (Brand: Cadbury)
  • 1/2 Tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 Tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 Tsp Bicarbonate Soda

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 180

  2. Step 2: 2. In a skillet, melt your butter over medium-high heat until it&#x27;s a rich brown colour and smells nutty

  3. Step 3: Pour butter into a separate bowl and add 1 tbsp of Monkfruit sweetener and mix through. Crack your egg into mixture and stir.

  4. Step 4: Add the flour, baking powder and bicarbonate soda and give it a mix. Finally, add your vanilla extract and chocolate chips.

  5. Step 5: Add cookie batter back into skillet and bake at 180 for 10-14 minutes depending on how gooey you like your cookies.
