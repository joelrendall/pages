Meatball Pasta Bake (4 Servings)

25 minutes 454 kcal • 50g protein • 10g fat • 38g carbs

Original yield: 4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 500g Extra Lean Beef Mince
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Slice Low Calorie Bread [blended
  • into crumbs] (Brand: Simsons Pantry)
  • Salt, Pepper, Oregano & Chilli
  • Flakes
  • 1 Tbsp Minced Garlic
  • 250g Protein Pasta (Brand: San
  • Remo Pasta Pro)
  • Cherry Tomato, Mushroom,
  • Spinach, Capsicum & Spring Onion (or your choice of vegetables)
  • 200g Tomato Passata
  • 35g Low Fat Fetta Cheese

Steps

  1. In a bowl add 500g of extra lean beef mince with 1 egg, 1 blended slice of bread, salt, pepper, chilli flakes & 1 tbsp of minced garlic.
  2. Mix everything together & using your hands form the meat into small even balls.
  3. Cook the meatballs in oiled pan on medium heat and set aside
  4. Cook 250g of protein pasta while the meatballs are cooking.
  5. Once the vegetables are chopped add them to the same pan you used to cook the meatballs & add 200g of tomato passata with a dash of salt, pepper & oregano.
  6. Drain around 80% of the pasta water, then add pasta and remaining pasta water into the pan with the vegetables & mix it together.
  7. Once everything is mixed together pour it all into a big baking dish & bake, top with meatballs grill in the oven at 200 degrees celsius for 25 minutes.
  8. Once baked take it out the oven & crumble 35g of fetta cheese across the pasta dish then place it back in the oven for 5 minutes.
