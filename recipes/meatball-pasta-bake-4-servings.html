Meatball Pasta Bake (4 Servings)

25 minutes 454 kcal • 50g protein • 10g fat • 38g carbs

Original yield: 4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 500g Extra Lean Beef Mince
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Slice Low Calorie Bread [blended
  • into crumbs] (Brand: Simsons Pantry)
  • Salt, Pepper, Oregano & Chilli
  • Flakes
  • 1 Tbsp Minced Garlic
  • 250g Protein Pasta (Brand: San
  • Remo Pasta Pro)
  • Cherry Tomato, Mushroom,
  • Spinach, Capsicum & Spring Onion (or your choice of vegetables)
  • 200g Tomato Passata
  • 35g Low Fat Fetta Cheese

Instructions

  1. Step 1: In a bowl add 500g of extra lean beef mince with 1 egg, 1 blended slice of bread, salt, pepper, chilli flakes &amp; 1 tbsp of minced garlic.

  2. Step 2: Mix everything together &amp; using your hands form the meat into small even balls.

  3. Step 3: Cook the meatballs in oiled pan on medium heat and set aside

  4. Step 4: Cook 250g of protein pasta while the meatballs are cooking.

  5. Step 5: Once the vegetables are chopped add them to the same pan you used to cook the meatballs &amp; add 200g of tomato passata with a dash of salt, pepper &amp; oregano.

  6. Step 6: Drain around 80% of the pasta water, then add pasta and remaining pasta water into the pan with the vegetables &amp; mix it together.

  7. Step 7: Once everything is mixed together pour it all into a big baking dish &amp; bake, top with meatballs grill in the oven at 200 degrees celsius for 25 minutes.

  8. Step 8: Once baked take it out the oven &amp; crumble 35g of fetta cheese across the pasta dish then place it back in the oven for 5 minutes.
