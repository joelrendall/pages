Biscoff Weetbix

Ingredients

  • 2.0 weetbix
  • 60 ml of skim milk/milk alternative
  • 10 ml Biscoff spread
  • 160 g Yopro Vanilla
  • Yoghurt or Salted Caramel flavour
  • 1.0 biscoff Biscuit crushed

Instructions

  1. Crush Weetbix into a bowl, add milk and stir well. Let the Weetbix and milk mixture sit for a few minutes to thicken. Pat the Weetbix mixture down firmly to create the base.
  2. In a separate bowl combine yoghurt and 1 tsp Biscoff spread. You may need to melt biscoff spread to mix in properly.
  3. Pour the yoghurt mix into your bowl to cover the Weetbix base.
  4. Melt the remaining Biscoff spread in the microwave, then drizzle over the yoghurt mix.
  5. Top with the crushed Biscoff biscuit, then set in the fridge.
