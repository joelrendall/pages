Teriyaki Salmon Bites

Ingredients

  • 130 g Salmon Fillet Skinless
  • 30 ml Soy Sauce (Woolworths)
  • 5 g Ginger
  • 5 g Garlic
  • 2 ml Lemon
  • 20 g Maple Syrup Sugar Free (Queen)
  • 1 g Chilli Flakes
  • 1 g Sesame Seeds
  • 100 g Bok Choy
  • 50 g Spring Onion
  • 100 g Rice Cooked (Any)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl mix the Soy sauce, lemon juice, crushed Ginger and Garlic, Chilli and a pinch of salt
  2. 2. Cut the salmon into bite size pieces and put in the marinade, leave in the fridge for 20-30 min
  3. Cook your rice in a rice cooker or a pot on the stove
  4. Take the fish out of the marinade and set aside, poor the marinade into a frypan and add the maple, turn the heat on and let this sauce reduce by half
  5. Place the fish into the reduced sauce and cook for 8-10 minutes or the fish is cook almost all the way through making sure you are turning the fish on all sides so it is glazed evenily
  6. Blanch the Bok Choi in a pot of salted boiling water
  7. Plate the cooked Fish and Bok Choi drizzling any sauce left in the pan over the fish
  8. Garnish with the spring onions and Sesame .
