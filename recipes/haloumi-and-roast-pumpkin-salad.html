Haloumi & Roast Pumpkin Salad

20 minutes 395 kcal • 15g protein • 23g fat • 37g carbs

Ingredients

  • 50g Cucumber
  • 50g Onion
  • 50g cherry tomatoes
  • 15g pine nuts
  • 40g Balsamic Vinegar Fat Free
  • 10g Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 200g Butternut Pumpkin
  • 50g Halloumi Cheese
  • 50g Lettuce

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 180c

  2. Step 2: Dice pumpkin into bite size pieces and lay on baking tray, season with salt and pepper and bake in oven for 20 minutes (or until soft)

  3. Step 3: Slice and cook halloumi in pan until golden brown

  4. Step 4: For your vinaigrette, stir balsamic vinegar into olive oil and set aside

  5. Step 5: Cut the rest of the veg and assemble the salad, dressing with your vinaigrette and garnish with pine nuts for a crunch!
