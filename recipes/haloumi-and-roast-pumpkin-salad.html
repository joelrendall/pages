Haloumi & Roast Pumpkin Salad

Ingredients

  • 50 g Cucumber
  • 50 g Onion
  • 50 g cherry tomatoes
  • 15 g pine nuts
  • 40 g Balsamic Vinegar Fat Free
  • 10 g Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 200 g Butternut Pumpkin
  • 50 g Halloumi Cheese
  • 50 g Lettuce

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180c
  2. Dice pumpkin into bite size pieces and lay on baking tray, season with salt and pepper and bake in oven for 20 minutes (or until soft)
  3. Slice and cook halloumi in pan until golden brown
  4. For your vinaigrette, stir balsamic vinegar into olive oil and set aside
  5. Cut the rest of the veg and assemble the salad, dressing with your vinaigrette and garnish with pine nuts for a crunch!
