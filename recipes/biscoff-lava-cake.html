Biscoff Lava Cake

2 minutes379 kcal • 36g protein • 12g fat • 33g carbs

Ingredients

  • 30 g protein powder
  • 24 g Powdered Peanut Butter With Cocoa (or regular powdered peanut butter)
  • 2 g Baking Powder
  • 100 ml Unsweetened Almond Milk
  • 1.0 crushed Biscoff Biscuit
  • 15 g Biscoff Spread (melted in microwave)

Instructions

  1. Step 1: In a bowl add 1 serving of chocolate protein powder, 2g of baking powder, 24g of powdered peanut butter with cocoa & mix the dry ingredients together.

  2. Step 2: Pour 100ml of unsweetened almond milk into the bowl then mix the dry & wet ingredients together to combine . 3. Pour the mixture into a bowl & microwave for around 1-2 minutes.

  3. Step 3: Add 1 crushed biscoff biscuit on top then drizzle 15g of melted biscoff spread & enjoy!
