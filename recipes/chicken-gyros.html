Chicken Gyros

17 minutes491 kcal • 49g protein • 6g fat • 53g carbs

Ingredients

  • 1.0 flatbread /Pita Bread
  • 130 g Raw Chicken Breast Grated Cucumber
  • 200 g Potatoes
  • 15 g Chopped Onion
  • 2.0 sliced Cherry Tomatoes Lettuce Salt, Dill, Pepper, All In 1 Seasoning, Garlic Herb Seasoning
  • 15 ml Lemon Juice

Instructions

  1. Step 1: In a bowl, mix together 80g of greek yoghurt, grated cucumber (amount is up to you, used a tiny bit for taste & texture), 1 tbsp of lemon juice, salt, dill, & pepper.

  2. Step 2: On a seperate plate season 130g of chicken breast with garlic herb seasoning on each side (or seasonings of choice) & cook the chicken using a non stick pan on high heat. (or you can use a regular pan with oil / the air fryer)

  3. Step 3: Cut 200g of potatoes into thick french fry shapes, season with all in 1 seasoning & bake in the air fryer at 180 degrees celsius for 15-17 minutes. (or in the oven for 25-30 minutes)

  4. Step 4: Using a pan lightly toast the flatbread/pita bread for 30 seconds on each side on low heat (optional)

  5. Step 5: Spread 1/2 of the tzatziki sauce on the bread then add lettuce (as much as you like), 15g chopped onion, 2 sliced cherry tomatoes, baked potato chips, chicken & top with the other half of the tzatziki sauce.

  6. Step 6: Tightly roll bread from one end to the other.
