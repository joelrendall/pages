Beef Nachos

Ingredients

  • Low Calorie Wrap/Tortilla (Mission Low Gi+Protein Wrap)
  • 130 g Extra Lean Beef Mince (115g Cooked)
  • 25 g Low Fat Cheese (Beqa 50% Less Fat Cheese)
  • 30 ml Lite Sour Cream
  • 5 ml Salsa
  • 0.5 tomao (Diced)
  • 10 g Red Onion
  • 10 g Capsicum/Chilli Pepper
  • Paprika, Garlic Salt , Mexican Seasoning
  • 5 ml Parsley Flakes
  • Lemon Juice
  • Avocado Oil/Low Calorie Cooking
  • Spray (Optional)
  • Spring Onions (garnish)

Instructions

  1. Start by spraying the wrap with a tiny bit of avocado oil/low calorie cooking spray.
  2. Season oiled wrap with paprika and garlic salt then slice into triangles and bake in air fryer at 180 for 3 minutes (5 mins if in oven) for tortilla chips.
  3. Cook 130g of extra lean beef mince on high heat & season with 1 tsp of mexican seasoning & 1 tsp of salsa.
  4. Put cooked mince and tortilla chips in bowl, then atop with 25g low fat cheese and bake until cheese is melted.
  5. Meanwhile, in a small separate bowl mix 1/2 diced tomato, 10g of diced red onion, 10g of diced capsicum, 1 tsp of parsley flakes & a squeeze of lemon juice
  6. Add the toppings you just mixed together from step 5 on top of nachos.
  7. Drizzle 2 Tbsp of lite sour cream over the nachos, garnish with spring chopped spring onions.
