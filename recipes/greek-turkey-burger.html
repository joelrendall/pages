Greek Turkey Burger

Ingredients

  • 150 g Turkey Breast Mince
  • 15 ml Diced Onion Smoked
  • Paprika BBQ Seasoning or SOC
  • 25 g Greek Yoghurt
  • Grated Cucumber Lemon Juice Dill
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 1.0 slice Tomato
  • Sliced Onion Spinach
  • 1.0 gourmet Brioche Bun
  • 10 g Reduced Fat Greek Style Feta (South Cape)

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Start by placing 150g of turkey breast mince into a bowl & mix with 1 tbsp of diced onion, smoked paprika & BBQ seasoning.

  2. Step 2: With your hands, form the seasoned turkey mince into a patty.

  3. Step 3: In a separate bowl mix 25g of greek yoghurt, a tiny bit of grated cucumber, a drop of lemon juice, dill, salt & pepper.

  4. Step 4: Turn the stove on high heat & cook the turkey patty on a pan for a few minutes on each side or until brown.

  5. Step 5: Just before the patty is fully cooked place each brioche bun on the pan as well.

  6. Step 6: Construction time! Build burger in this order: turkey burger, tzatziki, sliced onion, spinach and some crumbled feta. Replace the stop bun and enjoy!
