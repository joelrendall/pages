Brekky Pizza

10 minutes391 kcal • 33g protein • 8g fat • 46g carbs

Ingredients

  • 1.0 large Wholemeal Pita Bread
  • 2.0 short Cut Bacon Rashers
  • 40 g Leggo's Pizza Sauce
  • 25 g Light Mozzarella
  • 20 g Spinach
  • 8.0 cherry Tomatoes Halved
  • 1.0 egg
  • 1.0 extra Lean Beef Sausage (Brand: Peppercorn Food Company Fresh basil

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Preheat your oven to 220, also leave tray in oven to pre heat as well.

  2. Step 2: Slice sausage and shortcut bacon into small pieces and cook both in fry pan

  3. Step 3: Start making pizza by spreading pizza sauce across pita bread, leaving some room at the edges

  4. Step 4: Add cooked bacon, sausage, spinach and cherry tomatoes to the party. Leave an egg sized well in the middle of the pizza.

  5. Step 5: Crack egg in the well (duh), and sprinkle cheese all over the pizza

  6. Step 6: Pop pizza in oven on pre heated tray and cook for 7- 10 minutes or until egg white is cooked through.

  7. Step 7: Garnish with chopped fresh basil and enjoy!
