Kung Pao Chicken 5 Servings

30 minutes 340 kcal • 32g protein • 6g fat • 35g carbs

Original yield: 5 Servings

Ingredients

  • 600g Chicken Breast
  • 2 Cups White Rice (uncooked)
  • 1 Capsicum Chopped
  • 3 Spring Onions Chopped
  • 1 tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Minced Garlic
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 110 ml Light Soy
  • 1 Tbsp Sesame Oil
  • 1 Tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 Tsp Salt
  • 1 Tbsp Hoisin Sauce
  • 20g Cornflour
  • 10g Honey (optional) 5g Crushed Peanuts

Steps

  1. Dice chicken breast into 1 inch pieces and season with garlic powder, 30ml of light soy sauce, 1 tbsp of sesame oil, salt and pepper then mix
  2. 2. Cook chicken on pan until golden brown and set chicken aside.
  3. On same pan add 1 tbsp minced garlic, 1 tsp of ginger, chopped capsicum, chopped spring onion, 80ml light soy sauce and 1 tbsp of hoisin sauce.
  4. Add cooked chicken back to pan and let it all simmer until thick. Add chopped peanuts if you have them!
  5. Serve chicken over cooked rice and separate into 5 containers for meal prep!
