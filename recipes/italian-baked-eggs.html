Italian Baked Eggs

Ingredients

  • Can of Italian Peeled Tomatoes
  • 4 x Eggs
  • Handful of Spinach
  • 20 g Pecorino Romano grated
  • 20 g Grated Mozzarella
  • Fresh herbs of choice (I use Basil and Parsley)
  • 2 x Wholemeal Bread
  • 2 x Garlic Cloves crushed
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Garlic and Herb seasoning

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Cook down crushed garlic, spinach and chilli flakes in a pan on medium heat until spinach has wilted.

  2. Step 2: Pour in can of Italian peeled tomatoes and break them up with spatula; season with salt, pepper and garlic and herb seasoning.

  3. Step 3: Create 4 wells and crack eggs into each, then sprinkle pecorino romano and mozzarella on top.

  4. Step 4: Bake in the oven @180for 10-15 minutes (or until eggs are cooked to liking)

  5. Step 5: Top with your fresh herbs and enjoy with some toast.
