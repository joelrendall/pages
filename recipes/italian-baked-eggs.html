Italian Baked Eggs

15 minutes 457 kcal • 39g protein • 30g fat • 45g carbs

Ingredients

  • Can of Italian Peeled
  • Tomatoes (Brand: Annalisa)
  • 4 x Eggs
  • Handful of Spinach
  • 20g Pecorino Romano grated
  • 20g Grated Mozzarella
  • Fresh herbs of choice (I use
  • Basil and Parsley)
  • 2 x Wholemeal Bread
  • 2 x Garlic Cloves crushed
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Garlic and Herb seasoning (Brand: Masterfoods)

Steps

  1. Cook down crushed garlic, spinach and chilli flakes in a pan on medium heat until spinach has wilted.
  2. Pour in can of Italian peeled tomatoes and break them up with spatula; season with salt, pepper and garlic and herb seasoning.
  3. Create 4 wells and crack eggs into each, then sprinkle pecorino romano and mozzarella on top.
  4. Bake in the oven @180for 10-15 minutes (or until eggs are cooked to liking)
  5. Top with your fresh herbs and enjoy with some toast.
