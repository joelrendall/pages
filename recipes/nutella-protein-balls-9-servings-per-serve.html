Nutella Protein Balls 9 Servings (per serve)

Ingredients

  • 60 g oats
  • 2.0 scoops Vanilla Protein Powder
  • 80 g Nutella
  • 20 ml Unsweetened Almond Milk
  • Toppings (Optional)
  • melted nutella (put in microwave for 10 seconds)
  • Sprinkle of Oat Flour

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients (aside from toppings) into a large mixing bowl and stir together until thick (slowly add more milk if required)
  2. Roll mixture into 9 evenly sized balls (should be 30g each) and line on tray or large plate
  3. Drizzle melted nutella over each ball and sprinkle with oat flour. Feel free to get creative with toppings.
  4. Pop in freezer for 1-2 hours and enjoy!
  5. Note: Keep stored in fridge or freezer
