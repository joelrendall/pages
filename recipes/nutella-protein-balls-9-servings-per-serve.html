Nutella Protein Balls 9 Servings (per serve)

10 minutes 103 kcal • 7g protein • 4g fat • 8g carbs

Original yield: 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 60g Oat Flour (Blended Oats)
  • 2 Scoops Vanilla Protein Powder
  • 80g Nutella
  • 20ml Unsweetened Almond Milk
  • Toppings (Optional)
  • 1 serving melted nutella (put in
  • microwave for 10 seconds)
  • Sprinkle of Oat Flour

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Add all ingredients (aside from toppings) into a large mixing bowl and stir together until thick (slowly add more milk if required)

  2. Step 2: Roll mixture into 9 evenly sized balls (should be 30g each) and line on tray or large plate

  3. Step 3: Drizzle melted nutella over each ball and sprinkle with oat flour. Feel free to get creative with toppings.

  4. Step 4: Pop in freezer for 1-2 hours and enjoy!

  5. Step 5: Note: Keep stored in fridge or freezer
