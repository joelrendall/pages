Vietnamese Beef Salad Serves 4

30 minutes396 kcal • 32g protein • 11g fat • 38g carbs

Ingredients

  • 500 g Beef Strips
  • 1.0 large packet of Rocket
  • 200 g Vermicelli Rice Noodles Sliced Snow Peas Juice of 1 Lime
  • 15 ml oil Marinade
  • 45 ml oyster sauce
  • 10 ml fish sauce
  • 2.0 garlic cloves crushed
  • 5 ml black pepper Pickled red onion
  • 0.5 red onion finely sliced
  • 15 ml rice vinegar
  • 5 ml sugar

Instructions

  1. Step 1: To make the marinade, mix ingredients together in large bowl. Add beef mince to bowl and allow to marinade for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Step 2: 2. For pickled red onion, toss ingredients together and set aside.

  3. Step 3: Soak noodles in boiling water until they soften, drain and set aside.

  4. Step 4: Blanch snow peas in boiling water for 1 minute, drain and set aside.

  5. Step 5: Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or frying pan over high heat. Add beef and stir fry for 2-3 minutes or until just cooked,

  6. Step 6: Combine rocket, noodles, snow peas and lime juice in large serving bowl. Add beef and drained pickled red onion slices, toss until combined and serve warm.
