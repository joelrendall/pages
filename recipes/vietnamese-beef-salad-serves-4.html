Vietnamese Beef Salad Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 500 g Beef Strips
  • 1.0 large packet of Rocket
  • 200 g Vermicelli Rice Noodles
  • Sliced Snow Peas
  • Juice of 1 Lime
  • 15 ml oil Marinade
  • 45 ml oyster sauce
  • 10 ml fish sauce
  • 2.0 garlic cloves crushed
  • 5 ml black pepper
  • Pickled red onion
  • 0.5 red onion finely sliced
  • 10 g rice vinegar
  • 4 g sugar

Instructions

  1. To make the marinade, mix ingredients together in large bowl. Add beef mince to bowl and allow to marinade for at least 30 minutes.
  2. 2. For pickled red onion, toss ingredients together and set aside.
  3. Soak noodles in boiling water until they soften, drain and set aside.
  4. Blanch snow peas in boiling water for 1 minute, drain and set aside.
  5. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or frying pan over high heat. Add beef and stir fry for 2-3 minutes or until just cooked,
  6. Combine rocket, noodles, snow peas and lime juice in large serving bowl. Add beef and drained pickled red onion slices, toss until combined and serve warm.
