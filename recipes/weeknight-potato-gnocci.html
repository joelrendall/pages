Weeknight Potato Gnocci

Ingredients

  • 160 g Gnocchi Di Patate
  • 200 g Canned Diced Tomatoes
  • 60 g Onion
  • 1.0 clove Garlic
  • 2.0 sprigs Rosemary
  • 20 g Cheese (Parmesan)
  • 10 g Basil
  • 5 g Olive Oil

Instructions

  1. Put a pot of salted water on the stove and bring to a boil
  2. Slice your onions and crush your garlic
  3. In a large frying pan saut√© your onions, garlic and rosemary in a small amount of olive oil seasoned with salt and pepper
  4. Once the onions are cooked add in your tinned tomatoes and continue to cook for 10 to 15 minutes
  5. Cook your gnocchi in the boiling water for 4 to 5 minutes, Drain well and pour into your tomato sauce
  6. Pour gnocchi and sauce combination on to plate and garnish with parmesan and fresh basil.
