Weeknight Potato Gnocci

15 minutes482 kcal • 15g protein • 13g fat • 76g carbs

Ingredients

  • 160 g Gnocchi Di Patate
  • 200 g Canned Diced Tomatoes
  • 60 g Onion
  • 1.0 clove Garlic
  • 2.0 sprigs Rosemary
  • 20 g Cheese (Parmesan)
  • 10 g Basil
  • 5 g Olive Oil

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Put a pot of salted water on the stove and bring to a boil

  2. Step 2: Slice your onions and crush your garlic

  3. Step 3: In a large frying pan sauté your onions, garlic and rosemary in a small amount of olive oil seasoned with salt and pepper

  4. Step 4: Once the onions are cooked add in your tinned tomatoes and continue to cook for 10 to 15 minutes

  5. Step 5: Cook your gnocchi in the boiling water for 4 to 5 minutes, Drain well and pour into your tomato sauce

  6. Step 6: Pour gnocchi and sauce combination on to plate and garnish with parmesan and fresh basil.
