5 Servings

Ingredients

  • 600 g Chicken Breast 2 Cups White Rice (uncooked)
  • 1.0 capsicum Chopped
  • 3.0 spring Onions Chopped
  • 8 g Garlic Powder
  • 15 ml Minced Garlic
  • 5 ml ginger
  • 110 ml Light Soy
  • 15 ml Sesame Oil
  • 5 ml Black Pepper
  • 5 ml Salt
  • 15 ml Hoisin Sauce
  • 20 g Cornflour
  • 10 g Honey (optional) 5g Crushed Peanuts

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Dice chicken breast into 1 inch pieces and season with garlic powder, 30ml of light soy sauce, 1 tbsp of sesame oil, salt and pepper then mix

  2. Step 2: 2. Cook chicken on pan until golden brown and set chicken aside.

  3. Step 3: On same pan add 1 tbsp minced garlic, 1 tsp of ginger, chopped capsicum, chopped spring onion, 80ml light soy sauce and 1 tbsp of hoisin sauce.

  4. Step 4: Add cooked chicken back to pan and let it all simmer until thick. Add chopped peanuts if you have them!

  5. Step 5: Serve chicken over cooked rice and separate into 5 containers for meal prep!
