BBQ Meat Lovers Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1.0 large White Lebanese Bread
  • 60 g Extra Lean Beef Mince (45g cooked)
  • 30 ml Stubb's BBQ Sauce
  • 25 g Pizza Sauce
  • 35 g Low Fat Grated Cheese
  • 40 g Sliced Chicken
  • (57g) Extra Lean Beef
  • Chipolatas Oregano

Instructions

  1. Season and then cook beef mince in pan on high heat for a few minutes, once cooked put aside.
  2. Using the same pan cook 1 serving (57g) of extra lean beef chipolatas (sausages), slice then set aside.
  3. . Bake 1 large lebanese bread (or large tortilla wrap/pizza base) in the oven at 180 degrees celsius for 5 minutes.
  4. . Once the bread is slightly baked spread 25g of pizza sauce evenly across base then add toppings.
  5. Start by adding 35g of low fat cheese then add the extra lean beef mince, beef chipolatas (sausages), 40g of sliced chicken, & oregano on top.
  6. Bake in the oven again for 5-10 minutes at 180 degrees celsius or until the cheese is melted.
  7. Once you have taken it out the oven drizzle 2 tbsp of Stubb's BBQ sauce (or any low calorie BBQ sauce) across the pizza.
