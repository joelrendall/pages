BBQ Meat Lovers Pizza

5 minutes 490 kcal • 41g protein • 15g fat • 44g carbs

Original yield: 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1 Large White Lebanese Bread (Brand: Filla Bakery)
  • 60g Extra Lean Beef Mince (45g
  • cooked)
  • 2 Tbsp Stubb's BBQ Sauce
  • 25g Pizza Sauce (Brand: Leggo's)
  • 35g Low Fat Grated Cheese (Brand:
  • Bega)
  • 40g Sliced Chicken (Brand:
  • Thomson's Deli)
  • 1 Serving (57g) Extra Lean Beef
  • Chipolatas (Brand: Peppercorn the
  • Good Food Company)
  • Oregano

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Season and then cook beef mince in pan on high heat for a few minutes, once cooked put aside.

  2. Step 2: Using the same pan cook 1 serving (57g) of extra lean beef chipolatas (sausages), slice then set aside.

  3. Step 3: . Bake 1 large lebanese bread (or large tortilla wrap/pizza base) in the oven at 180 degrees celsius for 5 minutes.

  4. Step 4: . Once the bread is slightly baked spread 25g of pizza sauce evenly across base then add toppings.

  5. Step 5: Start by adding 35g of low fat cheese then add the extra lean beef mince, beef chipolatas (sausages), 40g of sliced chicken, &amp; oregano on top.

  6. Step 6: Bake in the oven again for 5-10 minutes at 180 degrees celsius or until the cheese is melted.

  7. Step 7: Once you have taken it out the oven drizzle 2 tbsp of Stubb&#x27;s BBQ sauce (or any low calorie BBQ sauce) across the pizza.
