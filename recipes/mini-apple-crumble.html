Mini Apple Crumble

Ingredients

  • 100 g Apple (small)
  • 15 ml Natural Sweetener (stevia)
  • 5 ml Cinnamon
  • 10 g Sugar Free Maple Syrup
  • 1.0 arnott 's Biscuit (any brand biscuits will work)
  • 2 g oats

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees celsius.
  2. Slice an apple into small cubes & remove the core.
  3. Place the cubed apple pieces into a bowl then add 1 Tbsp of natural sweetener (stevia), 1 tsp of cinnamon & 1 Tbsp of sugar free maple syrup.
  4. Mix everything together so that all of the apple pieces are covered completely.
  5. Pour the apple pieces into a small oven safe baking dish.
  6. Crush 1 Arnott's biscuit (or any biscuit) & sprinkle it on top of the apples pieces.
  7. Sprinkle 2g of oats on top for extra crunch (optional)
  8. Bake in the oven at 180 degrees celsius for 30 minutes.
  9. Add a scoop of halo top ice cream on top (optional)
