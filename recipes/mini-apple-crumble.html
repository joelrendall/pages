Mini Apple Crumble

30 minutes120 kcal • 1g protein • 2g fat • 27g carbs

Ingredients

  • 100 g Apple (small)
  • 15 ml Natural Sweetener (stevia)
  • 5 ml Cinnamon
  • 15 ml Sugar Free Maple Syrup
  • 1.0 arnott 's Biscuit (any brand biscuits will work)
  • 2 g Oats

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 200 degrees celsius.

  2. Step 2: Slice an apple into small cubes & remove the core.

  3. Step 3: Place the cubed apple pieces into a bowl then add 1 Tbsp of natural sweetener (stevia), 1 tsp of cinnamon & 1 Tbsp of sugar free maple syrup.

  4. Step 4: Mix everything together so that all of the apple pieces are covered completely.

  5. Step 5: Pour the apple pieces into a small oven safe baking dish.

  6. Step 6: Crush 1 Arnott's biscuit (or any biscuit) & sprinkle it on top of the apples pieces.

  7. Step 7: Sprinkle 2g of oats on top for extra crunch (optional)

  8. Step 8: Bake in the oven at 180 degrees celsius for 30 minutes.

  9. Step 9: Add a scoop of halo top ice cream on top (optional)
