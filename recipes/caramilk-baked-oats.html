Caramilk Baked Oats

Ingredients

  • 100 g Banana
  • 1.0 egg oats
  • 10 g Maple Syrup Sugar Free
  • 2 g Baking Powder
  • 2 g Salt
  • 25 g Cadbury Caramilk Chocolate

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Preheat your oven to 175

  2. Step 2: Place your oats, banana, egg, maple syrup, baking powder and salt into a blender

  3. Step 3: Blend until smooth and leave the batter to rest for 10 minutes

  4. Step 4: Pour into a oven safe dish

  5. Step 5: Push your pieces of Caramilk in to the batter

  6. Step 6: Bake in the oven for 20 min or until cooked through
