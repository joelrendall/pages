Caramilk Baked Oats

Ingredients

  • 100 g Banana
  • 1.0 egg
  • 40 g oats
  • 10 g Maple Syrup Sugar Free
  • 2 g Baking Powder
  • 2 g Salt
  • 25 g Cadbury Caramilk Chocolate

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 175
  2. Place your oats, banana, egg, maple syrup, baking powder and salt into a blender
  3. Blend until smooth and leave the batter to rest for 10 minutes
  4. Pour into a oven safe dish
  5. Push your pieces of Caramilk in to the batter
  6. Bake in the oven for 20 min or until cooked through
