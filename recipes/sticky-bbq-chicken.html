Sticky BBQ Chicken

Ingredients

  • 180 g Raw Chicken Breast (150g cooked)
  • 20 g Mustard
  • 30 g Corn Flakes (crushed)
  • 50 g Franks Hot Buffalo Sauce
  • 20 g Stubb's BBQ Sauce (or any low calorie BBQ sauce)
  • 4 g garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Slice 180g of raw chicken breast into even strips like shown in the photo above.
  2. In a bowl, add the chicken strips, dash of salt, pepper, garlic powder and mustard and mix until chicken is coated
  3. Crush 30g of corn flakes in a zip lock bag with same seasonings, then add to bowl and coat chicken in the mixture.
  4. Once each piece is marinated & coated bake in the air fryer at 180 degrees celsius for 15 minutes. (or oven at 200 degrees celsius for around 20 minutes)
  5. While the chicken is cooking in a bowl add 50g of franks hot buffalo sauce with 20g of stubb's bbq sauce & mix that together.
  6. Once the chicken is cooked soak each piece into sauce mixture one by one then place it back in the air fryer to bake for another 2-3 minutes & enjoy!
