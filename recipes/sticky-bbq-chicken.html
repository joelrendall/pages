Sticky BBQ Chicken

15 minutes 391 kcal • 50g protein • 6g fat • 29g carbs

Ingredients

  • 180g Raw Chicken Breast (150g
  • cooked)
  • 20g Mustard
  • 30g Corn Flakes (crushed)
  • 50g Franks Hot Buffalo Sauce
  • 20g Stubb's BBQ Sauce (or any low
  • calorie BBQ sauce)
  • 1 Tsp garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Slice 180g of raw chicken breast into even strips like shown in the photo above.

  2. Step 2: In a bowl, add the chicken strips, dash of salt, pepper, garlic powder and mustard and mix until chicken is coated

  3. Step 3: Crush 30g of corn flakes in a zip lock bag with same seasonings, then add to bowl and coat chicken in the mixture.

  4. Step 4: Once each piece is marinated &amp; coated bake in the air fryer at 180 degrees celsius for 15 minutes. (or oven at 200 degrees celsius for around 20 minutes)

  5. Step 5: While the chicken is cooking in a bowl add 50g of franks hot buffalo sauce with 20g of stubb&#x27;s bbq sauce &amp; mix that together.

  6. Step 6: Once the chicken is cooked soak each piece into sauce mixture one by one then place it back in the air fryer to bake for another 2-3 minutes &amp; enjoy!
