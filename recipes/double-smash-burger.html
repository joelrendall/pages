Double Smash Burger

Ingredients

  • 150 g Extra Lean Beef Mince
  • Aldi Brioche Bun
  • 0.5 onion Diced
  • 2.0 sliced Cheese (I used Emporium Selection Burger Slices from Aldi)
  • 5 ml . American Mustard
  • Sliced Pickles

Instructions

  1. Slice onions as thin as you can
  2. 2. Roll beef into 2 x 75g balls
  3. Drop ball on ripping hot pan, sprayed with a small amount of oil
  4. Add small handful of onions on top, and smash patty down flat onto pan. The flatter you get it, the better it will taste.
  5. Salt the top of the smashed patty & flip once you get some browning on the edges
  6. After flipping, pop your sliced cheese on top.
  7. Once cheese has melted, remove patty
  8. Repeat with second patty (or at the same time if grill/pan is big enough)
  9. Construct burger with the rest of the ingredients
