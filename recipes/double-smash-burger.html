Double Smash Burger

20 minutes 670 kcal • 67g protein • 16g fat • 55g carbs

Ingredients

  • 150g Extra Lean Beef Mince
  • Aldi Brioche Bun
  • 1/2 Onion Diced
  • 2 Sliced Cheese (I used Emporium
  • Selection Burger Slices from Aldi)
  • 1 Tsp. American Mustard
  • Sliced Pickles

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Slice onions as thin as you can

  2. Step 2: 2. Roll beef into 2 x 75g balls

  3. Step 3: Drop ball on ripping hot pan, sprayed with a small amount of oil

  4. Step 4: Add small handful of onions on top, and smash patty down flat onto pan. The flatter you get it, the better it will taste.

  5. Step 5: Salt the top of the smashed patty &amp; flip once you get some browning on the edges

  6. Step 6: After flipping, pop your sliced cheese on top.

  7. Step 7: Once cheese has melted, remove patty

  8. Step 8: Repeat with second patty (or at the same time if grill/pan is big enough)

  9. Step 9: Construct burger with the rest of the ingredients
