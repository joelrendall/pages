Balsamic Chicken Sanga

15 minutes 483 kcal • 44g protein • 14g fat • 46g carbs

Ingredients

  • 160g Boneless, Skinless Chicken
  • Thigh
  • Paprika, Garlic Powder, Salt
  • Woolworths White Bread (~80 Cals
  • per slice)
  • 15ml Balsamic Glaze
  • 1 Slice Swiss Cheese
  • Rocket

Steps

  1. Season chicken thighswith oregano, garlic salt, and smoked paprika (I would have also used onion powder, didn’t have any tho)
  2. 2. Cook on high heat for 5-7 min aside and let rest for 5-10 more min
  3. Assemble your sandwich with toasted bread, Rocket, your chicken, Swiss cheese, and balsamic glaze
