Balsamic Chicken Sanga

15 minutes483 kcal • 44g protein • 14g fat • 46g carbs

Ingredients

  • 160 g Boneless, Skinless Chicken Thigh Paprika, Garlic Powder, Salt Woolworths White Bread (~80 Cals per slice)
  • 15 ml Balsamic Glaze
  • 1.0 slice Swiss Cheese Rocket

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Season chicken thighswith oregano, garlic salt, and smoked paprika (I would have also used onion powder, didn’t have any tho)

  2. Step 2: 2. Cook on high heat for 5-7 min aside and let rest for 5-10 more min

  3. Step 3: Assemble your sandwich with toasted bread, Rocket, your chicken, Swiss cheese, and balsamic glaze
