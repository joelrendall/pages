Teriyaki Chicken Bowl

Ingredients

  • 130 g Raw Chicken Breast cubed
  • 80 g Cooked White/Brown Rice (Sunrise Basmati quick rice)
  • 60 g Capsicum slices
  • 30 g Light Soy Sauce
  • 5 ml Olive Oil
  • 150 g Cooked Cauliflower Rice
  • 30 g Green Beans (chopped)
  • 30 g Teriyaki Sauce

Instructions

  1. . Add 1 tsp of olive oil to a pan on high heat & cook the chicken breast. (7-10 minutes)
  2. Once the chicken is cooked add salt, pepper & the vegetables to the pan & mix everything together.
  3. Add 30g of teriyaki sauce & 30g of light soy sauce to the pan, mix everything together again & let it cook for another 5 minutes on medium heat.
  4. After 5 minutes add 80g of cooked rice & 150 of cooked cauliflower rice to the pan & mix everything together to combine.
  5. Add your choice of toppings (optional) such as sesame seeds or more vegetables & ya done
