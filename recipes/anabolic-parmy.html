Anabolic Parmy

Ingredients

  • 140 g Chicken Breast (weighed raw)
  • 20 g English Style Leg Ham
  • 30 g corn flakes
  • 35 g Egg Whites (or 1 egg)
  • 35 g Garlic, onion & herbs pizza
  • sauce (Leggo's)
  • 25 g Light Shredded Mozzarella
  • Cheese (Coles)
  • 4 g Garlic Powder
  • 5 ml Paprika
  • 5 ml Italian Herbs
  • Basil or parsley to garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by weighing 140g of chicken breast & beat to flatten. (I used a wooden dough roller)
  2. Crush corn flakes in zip lock bag with garlic powder, paprika, Italian herbs, salt and pepper
  3. Once crushed pour the corn flakes onto a plate & in a separate bowl add 35g of egg whites.
  4. Soak the chicken breast in the egg whites then place it on the plate with the blended corn thin & coat both sides evenly, this will create the coating for the chicken breast.
  5. Bake the chicken breast in the oven at 220 degrees celsius for 20 minutes. (flip over half way through at the 10 minute mark)
  6. Once the chicken is cooked atop with leg ham, pizza sauce and light mozzarella cheese in that order.
  7. Bake in the oven again at 220 degrees celsius for 5 minutes until the cheese has melted
