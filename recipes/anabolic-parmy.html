Anabolic Parmy

20 minutes378 kcal • 54g protein • 7g fat • 53g carbs

Ingredients

  • 140 g Chicken Breast (weighed raw)
  • 20 g English Style Leg Ham
  • 30 g corn flakes
  • 35 g Egg Whites (or 1 egg)
  • 35 g Garlic, onion & herbs pizza sauce (Leggo's)
  • 25 g Light Shredded Mozzarella Cheese (Coles)
  • 5 ml Garlic Powder
  • 5 ml Paprika
  • 5 ml Italian Herbs
  • Basil or parsley to garnish

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Start by weighing 140g of chicken breast & beat to flatten. (I used a wooden dough roller)

  2. Step 2: Crush corn flakes in zip lock bag with garlic powder, paprika, Italian herbs, salt and pepper

  3. Step 3: Once crushed pour the corn flakes onto a plate & in a separate bowl add 35g of egg whites.

  4. Step 4: Soak the chicken breast in the egg whites then place it on the plate with the blended corn thin & coat both sides evenly, this will create the coating for the chicken breast.

  5. Step 5: Bake the chicken breast in the oven at 220 degrees celsius for 20 minutes. (flip over half way through at the 10 minute mark)

  6. Step 6: Once the chicken is cooked atop with leg ham, pizza sauce and light mozzarella cheese in that order.

  7. Step 7: Bake in the oven again at 220 degrees celsius for 5 minutes until the cheese has melted
