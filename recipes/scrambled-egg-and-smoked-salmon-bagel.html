Scrambled Egg and Smoked Salmon Bagel

10 minutes 532 kcal • 31g protein • 21g fat • 50g carbs

Ingredients

  • 2 Egg (Extra Large)
  • 1 Bagel 90g (Abe’s)
  • 5 grams butter
  • 50g Smoked Salmon (Brand: Ocean
  • Blue)
  • 1 Salt & Pepper
  • Chopped Chives

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Cut the bagel into 2 and toast

  2. Step 2: Crack eggs into bowl and whisk

  3. Step 3: Add butter to pan on medium heat and pour eggs over top. Stir constantly until eggs are almost cooked to your liking then take off the heat and season with salt and pepper.

  4. Step 4: Serve the eggs on the toasted bagel and the smoked salmon on top of the eggs, garnish with chives
