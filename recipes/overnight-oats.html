Overnight Oats

5 minutes 391 kcal • 33g protein • 8g fat • 46g carbs

Original yield: 1 Serving

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Unsweetened Almond Milk
  • 40g Blueberries
  • 60g Chopped Strawberries
  • 50g Uncle Tobys Oats 1 Serving
  • Vanilla Protein Powder
  • 2/3 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/2 tbsp Queens Sugar Free Maple
  • Syrup

Steps

  1. Pour rolled oats, unsweetened almond milk and protein power into bowl or container and mix
  2. Leave in fridge overnight, the raw oats will absorb the milk and protein powder making them soft and tasty.
  3. In the morning, add blueberries, chopped strawberries then top off with a sprinkle of chia seeds and a drizzle of maple syrup.
