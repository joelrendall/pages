Overnight Oats

5 minutes391 kcal • 33g protein • 8g fat • 46g carbs

Ingredients

  • 120 ml Unsweetened Almond Milk
  • 40 g Blueberries
  • 60 g Chopped Strawberries
  • 30 g protein powder
  • 10 ml chia seeds
  • 7.5 ml Queens Sugar Free Maple Syrup

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Pour rolled oats, unsweetened almond milk and protein power into bowl or container and mix

  2. Step 2: Leave in fridge overnight, the raw oats will absorb the milk and protein powder making them soft and tasty.

  3. Step 3: In the morning, add blueberries, chopped strawberries then top off with a sprinkle of chia seeds and a drizzle of maple syrup.
