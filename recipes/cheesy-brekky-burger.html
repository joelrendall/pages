Cheesy Brekky Burger

15 minutes 422 kcal • 39g protein • 13g fat • 26g carbs

Ingredients

  • 100g Lean Beef Mince
  • 15g Mozzarella Cheese
  • Egg
  • 20g Leg Ham
  • Aldi Brioche Bun
  • Sriracha
  • Lettuce
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Form beef mince into 2 x 50g balls
  2. 2. Add first ball to a ripping hot pan, and smash down with spatula, then season and Mozzarella cheese
  3. Add another patty on top, and use your fingers to seal the edges and form one big patty
  4. Remove the patty once cooked through and cheese is melted and gooey
  5. To the same hot pan, add egg, leg ham and toast brioche buns
  6. Remove once egg and ham is cooked and buns are toasted
  7. Construction time! I used sriracha and added some lettuce and cracked pepper to top it off.
