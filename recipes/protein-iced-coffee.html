Protein Iced Coffee

5 minutes 138 kcal • 26g protein • 2g fat • 4g carbs

Original yield: 1 Serving

Ingredients

  • 1.5 Tbsp Nescafé Gold Instant
  • Coffee (Or your choice of coffee
  • powder)
  • 1 Serving Protein Powder (your
  • choice of flavor & brand.)
  • 2 Tbsp Natvia Gold Sweetener (brown sugar alternative & optional
  • for a sweeter taste)
  • 100ml Unsweetened Almond Milk
  • 1 Cup Ice

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Start by boiling a jug of water.

  2. Step 2: While the water is boiling, stir 1 scoop of protein power together with almond milk.

  3. Step 3: Grab coffee cup and add coffee powder

  4. Step 4: Pour boiling water into the coffee cup till it reaches about 1/3 of the cup &amp; mix well with a spoon.

  5. Step 5: . Add 1 cup of ice to the coffee.

  6. Step 6: Pour the protein and almond milk you mixed earlier into the coffee cup.

  7. Step 7: Add 2 Tbsp of Natvia gold sweetener. (Optional for the ones that love sweetness)

  8. Step 8: Add your choice of toppings or drink as it is.
